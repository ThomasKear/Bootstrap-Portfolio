About Me

Sterling Archer is the world's most dangerous secret agent and works for the International Secret Intelligence Service (ISIS).

He is 36 years old and his codename is "Duchess."

He is proficient in all of the skills one would expect of a spy of his caliber including mastery of weapons, driving, and the martial arts. His main interest in his job, however, is the jet-setting lifestyle it affords.

He is fond of drinking, "tactical" turtlenecks, and Burt Reynolds.
